Citroon

September 1, 2011

Pitch

Control your smartphone from the web

Citroon was a service/website that allowed people to control an Android smartphone and especially the SMS part.

Thanks to Citroon the user could send real SMS from the citroon.com website while using his/her phone plan.
(Note: at this time in France everyone had unlimited SMS)

Citroon had all the advantages of the SMS (universal) and all the advantages of the web (real time and real keyboard)

Design by Damien Balada

My role

It was my second startup, following Cosmoz, with lots of learning!
I was doing everything (as a solo founder once again)

Technologies: XMPP, Erlang, LAMP, JavaScript
