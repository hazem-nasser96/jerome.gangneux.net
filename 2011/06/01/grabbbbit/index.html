Grabbbb.it

June 1, 2011

Pitch

Images and video bookmarking

Grabbbbit was a copycat of ffffound with more functionalities, for example, grabbing videos.

The project has been started by Julie and Damien who proposed a design and a set of functionalities. Like ffffound, grabbbbit was invite only to allow keeping high level contributions.

After five year, grabbbbit has been shut down.

Design by Damien Balada

My role

Integrate the design and did the full development.
Grabbbbit had a bookmarklet to capture content from any website... I also created a full white label version (used for other projects)

Technologies: techno: Symfony 3, LAMP, Redis

The project is closed
