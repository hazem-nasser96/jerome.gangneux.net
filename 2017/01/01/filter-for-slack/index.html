Filter for Slack

Pitch

Filter for Slack adds a very important feature to Slack: filters!

Thank to Filter for Slack you will be able to mute specific bots/person on a per channel basis.
You won't get output from the bots/person you choose to filter anymore, no notification, nothing.

Of course it is easy to filter a bot/person, just click on the filter action in the channel (close to the name/icon). and to unfilter, just go to the room details panel and click on their name in the filter session to remove them from the filter.

Checkout the extension on the Chrome extension store
