filter for Slack

January 1, 2017

Pitch

Filter for Slack adds a very important feature to Slack: filters!

Thanks to Filter for Slack you will be able to mute specific bots/person on a per-channell basis.
You won't get output from the bots/person you choose to filter anymore, no notification, nothing.

Of course, it is easy to filter a bot/person, just click on the filter action in the channel (close to the name/icon). And to un-filter, just go to the room details panel and click on their name in the filter session to remove them from the filter.

Checkout the extension on the Chrome extension store
