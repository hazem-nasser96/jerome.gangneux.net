THTR

July 1, 2016

Pitch

Chat with Scapin, Cléonte, Jourdin & more

What if Molière had written this play at the age of the SMS/Messaging?

THTR offers a very interactive — and quite new — experience of plays reading. Immerse yourself in the play like you were talking this the characters. And if you don't know the next line, don't worry, we will suggest it to you (or at least give you a clue).

This project is closed.
