Whask

February 1, 2016

Pitch

Swipe your opinion right!

Just a swipe

In the classroom, with your friends, at the movies… With Whask, a swipe is enough to share your opinion: YAY or NAY. Be careful… once the Whask is open, you have only 10 seconds to reply, so people have to answer quickly.

My role

I was the CTO, I developed the two app (native iOS and Android) I used Swift 2.1 at that time

The all app used a SAAS stack including Parse.com and Firebase (and other AWS)

Design by Damien Balada
