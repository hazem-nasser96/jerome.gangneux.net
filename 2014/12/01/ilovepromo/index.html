i love promo

December 1, 2014

Pitch

Mobile Application to Swipe Shop trends and sales

I LOVE PROMO is the Tinder of sales (fashion mostly)

The App works with the famous swipe gesture and allows the user to discover good deals in shopping, you can also populate your wishlist very easily.

My role

As a CTO I developed both app (Android and iOS) using Parse.com plus a LAMP server

Multiple script were running server side to get the data from our partners (XML / CSV) All these data were normalized for our database, I was almost real time.

Design by Damien Balada
