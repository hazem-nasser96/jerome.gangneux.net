Mr Smartphone

July 1, 2015

Pitch

The only App to buy or sell your second hand iPhones

Mr Smartphone is a spin off of Budeal that is only for iPhones

My role

When we were a NUMA (the French accelerator — season 7) a lot of people advised us to make a specialized version of Budeal on our most growing segment. After looking at our statistics, and thought about an opportunistic strategy, we decided to create Mr Smartphone.

I developed the App in one month, so we would be ready for the new iPhone 6S hitting the market.

The App is on iOS only, objective-c, it shares most of its code base with Budeal

Design by Damien Balada

The project is closed
